Profile Summary

I am Data Scientist & Computational Physicist with expertise in Deep Learning, Computer Vision and Transfer Learning. I am experienced in large language model fine-tuning, RAG, as well as in representation learning (ViT, CLIP) and reinforcement learning (Q-learning, Monte Carlo). Within my academic career, I contributed to a number of disciplines: quantum many-body systems, nonequilibrium electron dynamics and attosecond nano-optics. Most recent contributions include lightwave valley polarization, Floquet time-crystals and superconducting Qubits. My doctoral dissertation on topological 2D materials was awarded the prize for best doctoral thesis. Besides research and teaching, I have held organizational, editorial, and leadership roles in various institutes.
